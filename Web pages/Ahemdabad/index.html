Best time to visit

The best time to visit Ahmedabad is during the winter season. The ` minimum temperature is about 5 degree Celsius and the maximum temperature is usually 20 degrees Celsius. It is an entirely different experience to check out open-air restaurants and road-side stalls during winters, as the weather is just apt for enjoying the beauty of nature. So, when you are planning a trip to Ahmedabad, prefer going during the period between October and March.
